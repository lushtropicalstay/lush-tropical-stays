General House Rules

No smoking inside rooms.
No loud noises, loud music, or parties in the apartment.
No illegal substances allowed on the premises.
No unregistered guests allowed.
No parties or events in the apartment.
No pets allowed.

Birthday parties and weddings may be arranged separately in collaboration with the Sales Department.

Smoking Policy

Smoking is not allowed inside the rooms. Designated smoking areas are available at the end of the pier.

Smoking is only allowed in designated outdoor areas.

Other Considerations

  • Please do not leave AC running with doors open.
  • Please turn off AC once leaving the apartment or checking out.
  • Please turn off lights, TV and ceiling fan once checking out.
Help conserve energy during your stay.

Room Cleaning

As part of the short-term rental contract, daily room cleaning is not offered.

The resort and the apartment are separate entities. Housekeeping, additional linens and towels will not be provided by the resort.

Housekeeping can be requested through the host for USD $49 per request.

Additional cleaning is available by request.

Garbage Disposal

Garbage can be disposed at the designated location outside the front of the apartment. Go outside at the front of the apartment, then to the left of the check-in area under a mango tree.
