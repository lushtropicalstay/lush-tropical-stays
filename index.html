About Lush Tropical Apartment

A traditional Caribbean seaside vacation experience.

We offer a traditional Caribbean seaside vacation experience. Tropical, relaxing, family friendly 1-bedroom apartment located in a 4-star resort with a myriad of fun and unique amenities at your disposal.

Not even a minute walk from your doorstep to 2 beaches on the property. Access to a large pool, hammocks, tennis court, basketball court, to name a few. There is also nanny service available for children and babies. Centrally located in Runaway Bay, St. Ann — Jamaica's garden parish.
