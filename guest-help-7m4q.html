🏠 Apartment

Is Wi‑Fi available inside the apartment?

Yes. Complimentary Wi‑Fi is available throughout the apartment. The apartment shares the resort's Wi‑Fi network, and the Wi‑Fi password will be provided on the day of check-in.

Does the apartment have air conditioning?

Yes. The apartment has air conditioning in both the bedroom and the living room.

Do I need to climb stairs to get to the apartment?

No. The apartment is located on the ground floor, making it convenient for elderly guests, families with young children, and guests with luggage.

Is there a balcony or terrace?

Yes. The apartment has terrace/balcony areas at both the front and rear, providing comfortable outdoor seating.

How many beds are in the apartment?

The apartment comfortably accommodates up to three guests and includes:

  • Bedroom: One king-size bed
  • Living Room: One sofa/twin-size bed

This layout is ideal for couples, small families, or up to three guests.

Is there a kitchen?

Yes. The apartment includes a fully equipped kitchen should you wish to prepare your own meals.

🏖 Resort & Amenities

Will we have access to the resort?

Yes. Guests have access to many resort amenities including the main beach, eastern beach access, swimming pool, tennis court, basketball court, gardens, and walking paths. Some services require additional payment through the Front Desk.

Is there beach access?

Yes. Guests have access to the main beach and an eastern gate leading to a longer stretch of beach.

Is there a swimming pool?

Yes. Guests have access to the resort's large swimming pool.

Is there a waterslide?

Yes. The resort features the Big Blue Waterslide beside the activity pool.

Is there a gym?

Yes. A Fitness Centre is available on the property.

Is there a spa?

Yes. The resort includes a spa and sundeck area.

🍽 Food & Dining

Are there restaurants on the property?

Yes. The restaurant is operated by the resort, not the apartment. The All-Inclusive Package costs USD $150 per person per day and includes breakfast, lunch, dinner, and beverages. Children under 14 years pay half price. Food may also be purchased from the resort's à la carte menu where available.

Can I cook?

Yes. The apartment includes a fully equipped kitchen.

👵 Elderly Guests & Families

Is the apartment suitable for elderly guests?

Yes. The apartment is on the ground floor, so no stairs are required for access.

Is the apartment family friendly?

Yes. Families are welcome. The resort also offers nanny services for an additional fee.

🚕 Transportation & Parking

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in: 3:00 PM
Check-out: 12:00 PM (Noon)

How do I collect the keys?

Please check in at the Front Desk of Franklyn D Resort & Spa. After check-in you will receive your apartment keys, resort wristband, and resort information if applicable.

How do I arrange airport transportation?

If arriving through Sangster International Airport, we recommend Jamaica Tours Limited. Visit https://jamaicatoursltd.com and select Franklyn D Resort & Spa to arrange your airport transfer.

Is parking available?

Yes. Free secure parking is available for guests staying at the resort.

Can you recommend local taxi drivers?

Yes. We are happy to recommend trusted local taxi drivers during your stay.

🛒 Shopping & Essentials

Where is the nearest supermarket?

The nearest supermarket is Emporium Supermarket, approximately a 7-minute walk from the apartment.

Is US dollars or Jamaican dollars accepted?

Most businesses accept both US Dollars (USD) and Jamaican Dollars (JMD). Credit and debit cards are also accepted at many locations.

Where is the nearest ATM?

There are several ATMs in Runaway Bay only a few minutes from the resort. The Front Desk can direct you.

Where is the nearest pharmacy?

Several pharmacies are located within Runaway Bay, only a few minutes from the resort.

Where is the nearest hospital or clinic?

For emergencies, the nearest major hospital is St. Ann's Bay Regional Hospital, approximately 20–25 minutes away by car.

🧹 Housekeeping & Laundry

How do I request housekeeping?

Daily housekeeping is not included. If you would like housekeeping, contact the host and we will arrange it with our housekeeper. Cost: USD $59 per request.

How do I arrange laundry?

We recommend T&S Elite Laundry & Cleaning Services. Services include wash, dry, iron, dry cleaning, pick-up and delivery. Telephone: (876) 574-9631. Address: Shop #12, Mom's Village Plaza, Runaway Bay, St. Ann.

👥 Visitors

Can visitors use the resort?

Visitors must comply with the resort's visitor policy. Please check with the Front Desk beforehand, as visitor access may be subject to resort rules and fees.

🤿 Activities

Is scuba diving available?

Yes. Scuba diving services are available on the property. Please ask the Front Desk about current prices. More information: https://www.scuba-jamaica.com

🚭 House Rules

Is smoking allowed?

Smoking is not permitted inside the apartment. Designated smoking areas are available at the end of the pier.

Are parties or events allowed?

No. Parties, events, loud music, and excessive noise are not permitted inside the apartment.

Are pets allowed?

No. Pets are not permitted.

⚡ Utilities

What should I do if there is a power outage or water interruption?

Power outages and temporary water interruptions can occasionally occur in Jamaica, particularly during severe weather. Remain calm, wait a few minutes, and contact the Front Desk or host if the issue continues.

📞 Contact

What if I still have questions?

Please contact us through your booking platform or using the contact information provided with your reservation.
