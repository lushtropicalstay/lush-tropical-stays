Guest Information Guide

LUSH TROPICAL APARTMENT

Welcome

Thank you for choosing Lush Tropical Apartment. We hope you enjoy a relaxing and memorable stay in Jamaica. This guide contains useful information to help you during your visit.

Transportation

Transportation can be arranged to destinations throughout Jamaica including:

  • Dunn's River Falls
  • Negril Seven Mile Beach
  • Rose Hall Great House
  • Montego Bay Highlights
  • Bob Marley Museum
  • Y.S. Falls

Recommended taxi and tour operators are available upon request. Please contact your host for current contact details and pricing.

Wi-Fi

Wi-Fi is available throughout the property. For security reasons, Wi-Fi credentials are provided inside the apartment and through your check-in information.

Property Information

  • Please keep doors and windows secured when leaving.
  • Switch off air conditioning when away from the apartment.
  • Respect quiet hours and neighboring residents.
  • Report maintenance issues as soon as possible.

Appliance Assistance

If the stove appears not to be functioning and no indicator lights are visible, check the pilot switch.

  • Up = OFF
  • Down = ON

Need Help?

If you require assistance during your stay, please contact your host through Airbnb messaging or your provided contact information.

Thank you for staying at Lush Tropical Apartment.
