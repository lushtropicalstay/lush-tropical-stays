General House Rules

No Smoking Indoors

Smoking is not allowed in the rooms.

No Loud Noise

No loud music, excessive noise or parties in the apartment.

No Illegal Substances

Illegal substances are not allowed on the premises.

Registered Guests Only

No unregistered guests are allowed.

No Events

No parties or events in the apartment. Birthdays and weddings may be arranged separately with the Sales Department.

No Pets

Pets are not allowed.

Smoking Policy

Smoking is not allowed inside the apartment.

Designated smoking areas are located at the end of the pier.

Other Considerations

  • Please do not leave the AC running with doors open.
  • Please turn off AC when leaving the apartment or checking out.
  • Please turn off lights, TV and ceiling fan when checking out.

Room Cleaning

Daily room cleaning is not included as part of the short-term rental contract.

The resort and the apartment are separate entities. Housekeeping, additional linens and towels will not be provided by the resort.

Housekeeping may be requested at USD $49 per request. Contact your host and the request will be relayed to the housekeeper.

Garbage Disposal

Garbage can be disposed of at the designated location outside the apartment.

  • Go outside at the front of the apartment.
  • Then go to the left of the check-in area, under a mango tree.

Restaurant & Bar on FDR Resort

The apartment and FDR Resort are separate entities.

Eating at the restaurant or drinking at the bar is only allowed if an All-Inclusive or Day Pass package is purchased at the front desk.

Please go to the front desk if you need assistance or more information on these packages.
